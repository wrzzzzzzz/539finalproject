負けヒロインが多すぎる！

Main Characters

やなみ あんな YANAMI

Major heroine of the series. A very pretty and kind high school student, whose hobby is delicacy cuisines. Be called「負けヒロインが」in Japanese, which means "Loser Heroine". Reason for this is that she has loved a boy for a long time, but she has never been able to confess her feelings until he fell in love with another girl.

ぬくみず かずひこ NUKUMIZU

Hero of the plot, who is always good at spotting the place for the most delicious tap water in the school building. Member of the literature club at school, but never showed up due to laziness (i.e., ghost member). He used to be accustomed to always staying on his own at school, until one day he witnessed and met YANAMI crying about her experience as a 「負けヒロインが」, his life started to change.

やきしお れもん YAKISHIO

Friend of YANAMI and NUKUMIZU. She is the memeber of the school athletics team, who is always practicing hard and making progress. Sharing similar experiences with YANAMI, she was once heartbroken, but quickly recovered and moved on. As a very positive and energetic person, she is always there to support and encourage all of her friends.

こまり ちか KOMARI

Member of the literature club, a very shy and emotional girl who likes reading novels. She used to love with the leader of the literature club, but never had the courage to confess her feelings. Despite this, she never gave up and always tried to support the club in her own way. Later on, together with NUKUMIZU, she bacame the leader of literature club and worked hard to make the club more active.
